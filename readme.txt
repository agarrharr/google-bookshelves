This plugin allows you to put a widget on your site that automatically displays the books in your Google Books 

== Description ==

With this plugin you can add a widget to your site that shows books from one of the shelves on your Google Books Library. You can choose to show your Reading Now, Favorites, Have Read, or To Read shelves. You can also choose the maximum number of books that you want to display.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `googlebooks.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Put the widget in a sidebar
4. For the settings, you will need your id number from Google Books. Go to books.google.com
5. Click on My Library
6. Click on Favorites (or any of your shelves). The url should look something like this: http://books.google.com/books?as_coll=3&num=10&uid=113720634485746776434&source=gbs_slider_cls_metadata_3_mylibrary_title
7. Copy the number after uid=
8. Go back to your Wordpress settings and paste that number into the settings of the Google Books Widget under ID Number.
9. Choose your shelf
10. Choose the maximum number of books you want to show (The default is 10) and click Save
